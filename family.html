Family Life

My family and I are very close and they are a huge part of my life. My family members include my mom, Leigh, my dad, Mitch, my sister, Carly (16), and of course my dog Sam! Sam is a husky shepard mix who weighs 85 pounds. He's destroyed a few things around the house but we love him anyway. My family loves to try new restaurants, go skiing and watch Survivor together. While I'm the first person in my family to attend the University of Michigan, everyone has become huge Wolverine fans!
